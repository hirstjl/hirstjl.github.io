What is WebCT?



WebCT is a collection of WEB-based Course development Tools.

It allows instructors ( with a small amount of training) to create:

    • Secure course content pages
    • Threaded discussion groups
    • Chat rooms
    • Online quizzes and homework
    • Student and group project directories
For more information, take a look at the WebCT home page.
